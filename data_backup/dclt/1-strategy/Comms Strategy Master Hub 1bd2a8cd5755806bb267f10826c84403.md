### üìä **CORE COMPONENTS**

### [1. **Audience Segmentation**](Comms%20Strategy%20Master%20Hub%201bd2a8cd5755806bb267f10826c84403/%E2%80%94%20Audience%20Segmentation%20&%20Messaging%20Strategy%201c22a8cd575580dbae92e4d70d9ee90d.md)

- Based on the 2015 Member Survey and current field knowledge.
- Segments include:
    - **Engaged Donor Ella** (65+, legacy giver)
    - **Outdoor Enthusiast Olivia** (active user, not high giver)
    - **Conservation Volunteer Victor** (younger, action-oriented)
    - **Legacy Giver Linda** (planned giving, trust-building)
- Each persona includes:
    - Psychographics, goals, values
    - Preferred channels (print/email vs. social)
    - Calls to action
    - Messaging tone
    - Engagement tactics

### 2. **Whole Communities Framework**

- A 2x2 matrix mapping privilege and focus (Nature vs. People).
- Quadrants:
    - Upper Left (Privileged/Nature): Legacy donors
    - Lower Left (Less Privileged/Nature): EJ groups, outdoor workers
    - Upper Right (Privileged/People): Educators, cultural orgs
    - Lower Right (Less Privileged/People): Frontline communities
- Used to **evaluate equity gaps** in current outreach.

### 3. **Messaging Library**

- Tone ranges from legacy-focused to action-focused.
