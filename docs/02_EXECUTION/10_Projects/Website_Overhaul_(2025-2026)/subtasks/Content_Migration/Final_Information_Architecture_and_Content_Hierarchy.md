# Final Website Information Architecture & Navigation Hierarchy

## 1. Visit
For public audiences, Eco-Lifestyle Enthusiasts, and Young Families.

- Explore Preserves  
- CTA: Visit a Preserve  
- Ties to: Preserve Explorer Map  
- Events & Programs  
- CTA: Register / Attend  
- Segments: seasonal, guided hikes, community events  
- Seasonal Highlights (Optional)  
- CTA: What’s in bloom, migratory seasons, etc.

---

## 2. What We Do
Foundational explanation of your impact — core for Legacy, Pragmatic, and Philanthropic personas.

- How We Save Land  
  - Intro to Preserves, Easements, and Partner Transfers  
  - CTA: Choose Your Path (Inquire, Volunteer, Donate)

- Nature Preserves  
  - Definition, public access, map  
  - CTA: Visit | Donate

- Conservation Easements  
  - How it works, FAQ, myth-busting  
  - CTA: Inquire

- Land Donations & Sales  
  - Donation types, benefits, process  
  - CTA: Talk to Us

- Partner Transfers  
  - Case studies, regional impact  
  - CTA: Support this work

- Stewardship & Care  
  - Invasive species, restoration, trail work  
  - CTA: Volunteer

- Research & Partnerships  
  - Scientific credibility, collaboration  
  - CTA: Learn / Fund / Join

- Hunting Program  
  - Safe, respectful access  
  - CTA: Apply | Safety Tips | Donate

---

## 3. Take Action
For Action Volunteers, Eco-enthusiasts, and Philanthropic Champions.

- Volunteer  
  - Opportunities, Stew Crew, roles  
  - CTA: Sign up

- Protect Your Land  
  - Full Landowner Hub  
  - Includes:  
    - Landowner FAQs  
    - Inquiry form  
    - Success stories  

- Join / Renew  
  - CTA: Join  
  - Benefits, tiers

- Advocate / Spread the Word (Optional Future)  
  - Social media toolkit, story bank

---

## 4. Resources
Central info hub for learners, researchers, and the cautious.

- Maps & Planning Tools  
  - Greenprint, LIO, groundwater map

- Library  
  - PDFs, research, policy docs

- Blog / News  
  - Educational stories, donor spotlights, seasonal updates

---

## 5. About
Mission credibility and trust-building for all personas.

- Mission & Vision  
- History  
- Staff & Board  
- Annual Reports / Financials  
- Careers  
- Contact

---

## 6. Shop
Could live under Resources or Give if small.

---

## 7. Give
Major CTA area with its own entry point (styled button)

- Donate Now  
  - Streamlined gift form

- Ways to Give  
  - All methods, land gifts, recurring

- Legacy Giving  
  - Will/bequest info, family stories

- Donor Stories  
  - Impact narratives and testimonials

- Philanthropic Champions  
  - Recognition, challenge gifts, naming opportunities
