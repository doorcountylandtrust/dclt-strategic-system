<b><span style="font-size: 14pt;">The Door County Land Trust is looking to hire a Communications Coordinator!</span></b>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;">The Communications Coordinator helps raise awareness and grow support for the Door County Land Trust’s mission. Using web content, social media, emails, newsletters, short films, outreach events, and other avenues, this position is ideal for a creative, deadline-driven, collaborative, and organized storyteller who enjoys meeting new people, experiencing new places, and describing the ways in which people connect to the natural environment of Door County. The Communications Coordinator position spends the majority of time in the office, with infrequent reporting in the field as needed. The position periodically requires evening and weekend hours.</span>

<b><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Essential Duties:</span></b>
<ul>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Executes strategic communications plan to raise awareness and support for Door County Land Trust. Ensures broad-level integration of the organization’s conservation agenda goals including land protection, land stewardship and community engagement.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Works collaboratively to align public relations, donor relations strategies and communications.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Coordinates and collaborates with fellow staff for content selection and writing assignments.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Coordinates publication and distribution through a variety of channels including direct mail, print, web, social media, video, etc.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Writes, edits, designs and publishes ongoing stories of the Land Trust in collaboration with other writers including staff, volunteers and occasionally paid contractors.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Writes and publishes website, blog and eNews content</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Maintains, curates and creates a variety of content for social media accounts.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Assists with other external and internal communications duties as needed.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Designs and produces brochures, advertisements, posters, merchandise and other promotional materials.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Coordinates and maintains accurate details on programs and events across multiple channels.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Supports planning, promotion and execution of a variety of member, public, program and fundraising events.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Collaborates with staff to design and create signage, visitor information and educational materials for nature preserves.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Supports donor relations and assists with donor communications as needed.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Develops and maintains relationships with media contacts.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Measures and reports on the effectiveness of communications activities.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Maintains photo library and communications archive.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Manages inventory of stationery, letterhead, publications and merchandise.</span></li>
</ul>
<b><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Supervision and Supervisory Responsibilities</span></b>
<span style="font-size: 14pt;">The Communications Coordinator reports to and is supervised by the Development Director. </span><span style="font-size: 14pt;">The Communications Coordinator supervises and coordinates volunteers related to the implementation of some events/activities and communications as needed; as well as project supervision of graphic and web design, printers, mail services, etc.</span>
<span style="font-size: 14pt;">Approximately 70% of work focuses on communications. 30% of work encompasses a variety of administrative, membership, and fund development support.</span>

<b><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Qualifications:</span></b>
<ul>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Three to five years of experience in communications, journalism, marketing, branding, or related career, preferably in the field of land conservation. Candidates are invited to demonstrate professional and related life experience.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">A passion for our mission to ‘Protect Door County’s Exceptional Land and Waters…Forever.’</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Bachelor’s degree or equivalent communications skills.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Experience collaborating with team members, colleagues and contractors.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Must possess strong verbal and written communications skills, have experience with copyediting, and maintaining high standards for accuracy.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Effective communication skills (including presentation, persuasion, and negotiation) required for working with colleagues, stakeholders, and contractors.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Adept in graphic design for print and digital media.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Adept with Microsoft Office Suite including Outlook, Word, PowerPoint and Excel.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Adept with Adobe Creative Suite including Photoshop, InDesign and Illustrator.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Proficiency in content management systems (WordPress), SEO, and Google Analytics desired.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Adept in email marketing systems and WYSIWYG editing (ConstantContact).</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Some experience in filming, editing, and sharing visual media (photos and video) desired.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Experience with Giftworks/Frontstream constituent database (or other database) desired.</span></li>
</ul>
<b><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Salary:</span></b>
<span style="font-size: 14pt;">This is a full-time position. Salary is commensurate with experience and includes competitive benefits. Health, life, and disability insurance. Simple IRA matching contribution. Paid vacation.
</span>

Follow instructions on the webpage to upload your resume, cover letter, references and work samples. Start date flexible, but ideally no later than January 15.</span>
