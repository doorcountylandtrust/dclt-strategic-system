<span style="font-size: 14pt;">Itching to get your hands dirty and really connect with the land? Discover the rewarding feel of restoration work in the field! Join the upcoming work party at White Cliff Nature Preserve and help with trail maintenance and land care. The day will include installing new trail marker signs while you hike through the beautiful trail system. People should dress for the weather and walking on uneven terrain (at least sturdy shoes and long pants). The Land Trust will bring all necessary supplies and equipment and of course our finest granola bars. Hope to see you there!</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Location: </strong>White Cliff Nature Preserve, 8248 White Cliff Rd, Egg Harbor, WI 54209</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Date:</strong> Tuesday, May 14, 2019</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Time:</strong> 9:00 am - 11:00 am*</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;">* Please arrive on time. Work crews meet at the trailhead area, but may be difficult to locate after work had begun.</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>What to wear:</strong> Dress in layers for a variety of weather possibilities. Long sleeves and long pants are recommended.</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>What to bring:</strong> Water bottle, sunscreen, snacks, insect repellent. You may bring work gloves, goggles, kneepads or other personal gear if desired.</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Cancellation Policy:</strong> Work day plans are dependent on weather. Please check for possible cancellations or schedule updates on the <span style="color: #3366ff;"><a style="color: #3366ff;" href="https://doorstewardshipalliance.weebly.com/">Door Stewardship Alliance website</a></span>.</span>
