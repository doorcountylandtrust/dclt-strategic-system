<h2>
		Membership and Giving
	</h2>
	<p><b>Support the <em>Door County Land Trust</em> today!</b></p>
<p>A donation to the <em>Door County Land Trust</em> helps protect Door County's exceptional lands and waters&#8230;<em>forever</em>. By making a donation to the <em>Door County Land Trust</em> you will join more than 4,200 other Land Trust supporter households who care about the current and future well-being of Door County.</p>
<p><b>Donations are used to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>permanently protect Door County's rural and natural heritage and its unique and fragile wildlife habitat;</li>
<li>educate the community about the importance of land protection; and</li>
<li>make land available to the public for hiking, cross-country skiing, canoeing, hunting, fishing, and the quiet contemplation of nature.</li>
</ul>
<p><a href="https://www.doorcountylandtrust.org/renew-my-membership/"><b>Renew My Membership</b></a><b> or <a href="https://www.doorcountylandtrust.org/join-the-land-trust/">Join the Land Trust</a></b></p>
<p>Membership dollars are used for land acquisition, stewardship and restoration on the nature preserves, volunteer projects, and educational activities and events. Begin your membership today with an annual or monthly gift.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.doorcountylandtrust.org/give-a-tribute-or-memorial-gift/"><b>Give a Tribute or Memorial Gift</b></a></p>
<p>When you make a gift to the Door County Land Trust honoring a friend, family member, a special event, or the life of a loved one, your gift lasts as long as the land does - forever!</p>
<p><a href="https://www.doorcountylandtrust.org/give-generously/membership-and-giving/stock-gifts/"><b>Stock Gifts</b></a></p>
<p>Making a gift of stock or securities is as easy as picking up the phone. <a href="https://www.doorcountylandtrust.org/give-generously/membership-and-giving/stock-gifts/">Click here</a> for necessary information for your broker.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.doorcountylandtrust.org/give-generously/planned-giving/" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><b>The Legacy Circle: Planned Giving</b></a></p>
<p>By letting us know of your intention to make a gift through your will, trust, annuity, insurance or other planned or deferred means, you become a member of the <em>Door County Land Trust </em><b>Legacy Circle</b>. Giving to the <em>Door County Land Trust</em> through your estate plan is a wonderful way to leave a legacy of honoring the natural world</p>
<p><a href="https://www.doorcountylandtrust.org/give-generously/membership-and-giving/business-membership/"><b>Business Membership</b></a></p>
<p>We invite you to join the many businesses that support the <em>Door County Land Trust</em> and become an Annual Business Member. As a Land Trust business member, you will be demonstrating your commitment to making a lasting difference in your community and the future of Door County.</p>
