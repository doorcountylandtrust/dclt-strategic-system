<h2 style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #333399;"><strong>A Holiday Nature Celebration</strong></span></h2>
<div>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;">Enjoy exploring along with our Twelve Hikes of Christmas, h</span><span style="font-size: 14pt;">opefully you will join our holiday celebration and get </span><em style="font-size: 14pt;">wild</em><span style="font-size: 14pt;"> out on the trails with us.</span>
