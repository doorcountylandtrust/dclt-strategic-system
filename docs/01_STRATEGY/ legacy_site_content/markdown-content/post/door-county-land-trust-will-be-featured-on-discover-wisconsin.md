<span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: georgia, palatino, serif;">On June 13th at 6:30pm CST, Discover Wisconsin airs the Wisconsin Land Trust Days episode on television stations across the Midwest. Tune in to watch wild mushroom expert Charlotte Lukes share her passion for conservation and fungi during the Fungus Among Us hike at <a href="https://www.doorcountylandtrust.org/kangaroo-lake-nature-preserve/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" data-link-type="web">Kangaroo Lake Nature Preserve</a>!</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: georgia, palatino, serif;">You’ll explore and learn about hidden gems protected by land trusts across Wisconsin. Stay tuned for the full episode...Door County Land Trust's Kangaroo Lake Nature Preserve is the fourth and final location featured in the episode! Tell your friends and watch it together -- from a safe distance, of course.</span>

&nbsp;

<strong><span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 14pt;">The episode airs on WFRV Channel 5 in Door County on Saturday, June 13, at 6:30 pm.</span></strong>
