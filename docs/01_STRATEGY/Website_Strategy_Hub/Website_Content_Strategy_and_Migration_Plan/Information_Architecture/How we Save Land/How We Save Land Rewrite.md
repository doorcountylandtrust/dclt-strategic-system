# Rewritten Content System: How We Save Land Section

This document contains the full strategic rewrites for the "How We Save Land" section of the Door County Land Trust website. It includes a page-by-page restructuring using modular content blocks, refined calls-to-action, and updated voice and tone guidelines to align with the organizational mission.

## Overview: How We Save Land

**Purpose:** Introduce DCLT's core land protection methods in a digestible, inspiring format.

### Content Blocks:

* **Hero Banner**: Inspiring imagery of Door County's natural beauty
* **Intro Text**: "Land protection happens in many forms — and each one leaves a lasting legacy."
* **Three Pillars (Icon Grid):**

  * **Nature Preserves** (DCLT-owned)
  * **Conservation Easements** (privately owned, permanently protected)
  * **Partner Transfers** (lands DCLT protects and transfers to others)
* **CTA**: "Choose Your Path" → Volunteer | Donate | Protect Your Land

---

## Nature Preserves

**Purpose:** Explain what preserves are, highlight accessibility, and connect to Preserve Explorer.

### Content Blocks:

* **Definition**: "Public lands permanently protected and owned by DCLT"
* **Map Preview** or Carousel of Preserve Photos
* **Benefits**: Public access, habitat protection, ecosystem restoration
* **CTA**: Visit a Preserve | Donate | Volunteer
* **Crosslink**: Preserve Explorer map | Events at Preserves

---

## Conservation Easements

**Purpose:** Empower landowners to protect their land in perpetuity without giving up ownership.

### Content Blocks:

* **What is a Conservation Easement?** Plain-language definition
* **How it Works**: Visual steps or FAQ accordion
* **Benefits**: Tax benefits, legacy protection, flexibility
* **Myths vs Facts** Block
* **Testimonial**: Quote from local landowner
* **CTA**: Protect Your Land (Inquiry form)

## Land Donations & Sales

**Purpose:** Guide landowners through gift or sale options and highlight legacy potential.

### Content Blocks:

* **Ways to Give Land**:

  * Full donation
  * Bargain sale
  * Bequest in will
* **Tax Benefits** Overview
* **Decision Tree** or Scenario Examples
* **Quote or Legacy Story**
* **CTA**: Inquire About Donating Land

## Partner Transfers

**Purpose:** Showcase conservation outcomes even when DCLT does not retain ownership.

### Content Blocks:

* **Definition**: Land protected then transferred to municipalities, parks, or other nonprofits
* **Notable Examples**: Grand View, Schwartz Lake, etc.
* **Benefits**: Larger scale protection, community impact
* **CTA**: Learn More | Donate to Future Projects
