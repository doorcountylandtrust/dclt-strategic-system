# Become a Member

*Date:* 2014-09-15 09:43:29
*Author:* dcltadmin
*Link:* https://www.doorcountylandtrust.org/give-generously/membership-and-giving/become-a-member/

**Join the *Door County Land Trust* today!**

As a member of the Door County Land Trust, you receive the following benefits:

* The Land Trust journal, ***Landings***, and other newsletters that highlight Door County's exceptional lands and waters
* Opportunities to explore Land Trust-protected properties
* Invitations to Land Trust-sponsored events
* Rewarding volunteer opportunities
* A connection with others who share a similar passion for Door County's wild places and open spaces
* And best of all, the satisfaction of being part of a solution to protect Door County's most important resource…the land itself!

In addition to the benefits listed above, contributors at the *Northern White Cedar Giving Circle,* *Grand Peninsula Society,* and *Bright Horizon Society* levels also receive invitations to special hikes and updates on the latest Land Trust news.

**Sign up for a one year membership available at the following levels:**

**Partner** ($35-$149)

**Preserver** ($150-$249)

**Steward** ($250-$499)

**Guardian** ($500-$999)

**Northern White Cedar Giving Circle** ($1,000-$9,999)

**Grand Peninsula Society** ($10,000+)

**Bright Horizon Society** ($20,000+)

 **Two Ways to Join:**

Annual Membership Contribution

To join, [donate online](https://www.doorcountylandtrust.org/join-the-land-trust/) or send us your [membership form](https://www.doorcountylandtrust.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/Membership_contribution.pdf) to make a donation at the membership level of your choice. Each year, you will receive a letter inviting you to renew your membership.

Evergreen Giving (Recurring Monthly Giving)

[Monthly giving](https://www.doorcountylandtrust.org/join-the-land-trust/) adds up! You can give your membership donation with a gift of any amount set to recur on a monthly basis.
