# —— Confirmation Email Details

Thank you for joining us for this important conversation. Below is a brief outline of what to expect during our upcoming session:

**1. Welcome & Introductions**

Meet our team, review community agreements, and share what you love about Door County.

**2. Your Connection to DCLT**

How you first heard about the Land Trust, why you got involved, and what you value most.

**3. DCLT’s Role in the Community**

Discussion about the challenges facing Door County and the role you think the Land Trust should play.

**4. Visual Moodboard Reactions**

Review 3 possible design directions and share what feels most (or least) like “DCLT” to you.

**5. Communication Preferences**

How you prefer to hear from us, what messaging resonates, and what doesn’t.

**6. Wrap-Up**

Final thoughts, what we’ve learned from you, and how your input will shape our work.

**What you’ll need:**

- A quiet space
- A computer or tablet with Zoom access
- Optional: Pen and paper for notes during moodboard review

**Questions?**

Contact Don Krumpos at [dkrumpos@doorcountylandtrust.org](mailto:dkrumpos@doorcountylandtrust.org)
