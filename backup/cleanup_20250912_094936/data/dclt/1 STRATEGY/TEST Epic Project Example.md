# Brand System (2026) - Strategic Epic

## Epic Vision

Transform DCLT's brand identity into a cohesive, modern system that enhances our conservation mission and strengthens community connections.

## Strategic Objectives

### 1. Brand Identity Excellence
- Develop comprehensive visual identity system
- Create consistent messaging framework
- Establish brand guidelines and standards

### 2. Digital Transformation  
- Modernize website and digital presence
- Implement user-centered design principles
- Optimize for accessibility and performance

### 3. Stakeholder Engagement
- Enhance donor and member experience
- Improve community outreach effectiveness
- Strengthen partnership communications

## Epic Timeline

**Phase 1: Foundation** (July - September 2025)
- Brand research and strategy development
- Visual identity system creation
- Initial stakeholder feedback

**Phase 2: Implementation** (October 2025 - January 2026)  
- Website redesign and development
- Marketing materials redesign
- Digital asset creation

**Phase 3: Integration** (February - March 2026)
- Staff training and adoption
- Community rollout
- Performance measurement

## Success Metrics

- **Brand Recognition**: 40% increase in unaided brand recall
- **Donor Engagement**: 30% improvement in engagement metrics
- **Digital Performance**: 50% improvement in website metrics
- **Internal Adoption**: 95% staff compliance with brand guidelines

## Risk Management

**High-Risk Factors:**
- Complex coordination across multiple projects
- Significant organizational change management
- External vendor dependencies

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Monthly steering committee reviews
- Phased rollout approach
- Change management communication plan
