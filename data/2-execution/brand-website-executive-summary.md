# Brand + Website: Executive Summary

*Updated: July 2025*

This executive summary provides a high-level snapshot of the strategy, goals, and current status of the Door County Land Trust brand refresh and website overhaul. It is intended for leadership, board members, and collaborators who need a quick understanding of direction, progress, and next steps.

This integrated project aligns our **visual identity** and **digital presence** with the current and future mission of Door County Land Trust. It is designed to:

- Modernize how DCLT communicates with members, donors, and visitors
- Improve usability, storytelling, and mobile performance across digital channels
- Increase membership conversion, donor retention, and public support
- Ensure our systems are maintainable, accessible, and future-ready
