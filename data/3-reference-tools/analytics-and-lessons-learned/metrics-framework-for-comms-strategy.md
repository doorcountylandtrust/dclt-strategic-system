# Metrics Framework for Comms Strategy

Metrics & Success Indicators

We’ll track impact through:

Website Traffic & Engagement – Growth in visitors, time spent, and donation conversions.

Email & Social Media Metrics – Increased engagement, shares, and donor conversions.

Fundraising Performance – Growth in donor participation and major gift contributions.

Volunteer & Membership Growth – Increased retention and new signups.

Public Awareness & Perception – Periodic surveys to gauge brand recognition and support.
