<h1><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><img class="alignnone wp-image-5130 size-full" src="https://www.doorcountylandtrust.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/02/2019-Water-Summit.jpg" alt="2019-Water-Summit" width="933" height="573" /></span></h1>
<b><span style="font-size: 24pt;">June 4th - 6th, 2019</span></b>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;">A year of celebration and education will culminate with a three day 2019 Celebrate Water Summit June 4-6, 2019. </span><span style="font-size: 14pt;">A year of celebration has lead to this: a three-day summit at the <a href="https://www.thelandmarkresort.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Landmark Resort</a> in Egg Harbor, featuring a keynote speaker, educational sessions, and field trips.</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;">For $35 you are able to enjoy three meals, your choice of 6 out of 12 educational sessions, a luncheon with author Dan Egan (<i>The Death and Life of the Great Lakes</i>), and a breakfast with Community Foundation CEO &amp; President, Bret Bicoy. The keynote presentation is held at the <a href="http://dcauditorium.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Door Community Auditorium</a> and is free and open to the public. Field trips are optional and presented for $20, which includes transportation. Included in the summit activities is a Science Poster Project that gives high school or college students an opportunity to present a project among their peers and professionals.</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;">You can reserve a room at <a href="https://www.thelandmarkresort.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">The Landmark</a> during the Summit by calling 1 (800) 273-7877. Request a room in the Celebrate Water block. Reservations are needed by April 20, 2019.</span>
