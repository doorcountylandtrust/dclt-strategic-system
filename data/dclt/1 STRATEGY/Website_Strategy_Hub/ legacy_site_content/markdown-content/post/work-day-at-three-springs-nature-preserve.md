<span style="font-size: 14pt;">Work day! Tuesday, August 20, 2019</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;">Itching to get your hands dirty and really connect with the land? Discover the rewarding feel of restoration work in the field! </span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;">Please join Door County Land Trust for a morning of work at Three Springs Nature Preserve. We will be using loppers and handsaws to cut and remove vegetation encroaching on the trail system.</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;">The Land Trust will bring all necessary equipment, but feel free to bring your favorite pair of work gloves or loppers if you would like. We will have water (bring your own water bottle) and granola bars on site.</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;">For more information, email:  <a href="https://www.doorcountylandtrust.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/05/workcrew-email.png"><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-5198" src="https://www.doorcountylandtrust.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/05/workcrew-email-300x26.png" alt="" width="300" height="26" /></a></span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Location:</strong>Three Springs Nature Preserve, 10442 Three Springs Road, Sister Bay</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Date:</strong> Tuesday, August 20, 2019</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Time:</strong> 9:00 am - 12:00 am*</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;">* Please arrive on time. Work crews meet at the trailhead area, but may be difficult to locate after work had begun.</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>What to wear:</strong> Dress in layers for a variety of weather possibilities. Long sleeves and long pants are recommended.</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>What to bring:</strong> Water bottle, sunscreen, snacks, insect repellent. You may bring work gloves, goggles, kneepads or other personal gear if desired.</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Cancellation Policy:</strong> Work day plans are dependent on weather. Please check for possible cancellations or schedule updates on the <span style="color: #3366ff;"><a style="color: #3366ff;" href="https://doorstewardshipalliance.weebly.com/">Door Stewardship Alliance website</a></span>.</span>
