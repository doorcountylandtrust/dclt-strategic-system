Tuesday, September 19, 9:00 am -

Join us to battle the Scots pine trees and seedlings that are invading the Three Springs Nature Preserve. Please meet in the parking area at the preserve at 9:00 am. We will be cutting seedling Scots Pine using loppers and piling the larger Scots Pine after it is cut. Please RSVP if you are able to join us!  volunteer@doorcountylandtrust.org or (920)746-1359

**Reminder - you must be chainsaw trained in order to operate any chainsaw (including your own) during a Land Trust volunteer event.
