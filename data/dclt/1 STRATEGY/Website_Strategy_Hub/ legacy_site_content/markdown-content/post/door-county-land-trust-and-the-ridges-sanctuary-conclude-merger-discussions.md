<h2 style="text-align: center;">Door County Land Trust and The Ridges Sanctuary Conclude Merger Discussions</h2>
<div><span style="font-size: 14pt; font-family: georgia, palatino, serif;"> The Ridges Sanctuary and Door County Land Trust decided to conclude their exploratory discussions for a possible merger. While the organizations determined now is not the right time to combine, they are committed to continue working together in various ways. Both organizations developed a deeper understanding of and appreciation for the other through this process.</span></div>
<div><span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 14pt;">
The community is fortunate to have several environmental partners, working in complementary ways to protect and preserve Door County’s critical habitats and scenic natural beauty.</span></div>
