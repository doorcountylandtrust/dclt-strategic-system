	<p><b>Join the <em>Door County Land Trust</em> today!</b></p>
<p>As a member of the Door County Land Trust, you receive the following benefits:</p>
<ul>
<li>The Land Trust journal, <strong><em>Landings</em></strong>, and other newsletters that highlight Door County's exceptional lands and waters</li>
<li>Opportunities to explore Land Trust-protected properties</li>
<li>Invitations to Land Trust-sponsored events</li>
<li>Rewarding volunteer opportunities</li>
<li>A connection with others who share a similar passion for Door County's wild places and open spaces</li>
<li>And best of all, the satisfaction of being part of a solution to protect Door County's most important resource&#8230;the land itself!</li>
</ul>
<p>In addition to the benefits listed above, contributors at the <em>Northern White Cedar Giving Circle,</em> <em>Grand Peninsula Society, </em>and<em> Bright Horizon Society</em> levels also receive invitations to special hikes and updates on the latest Land Trust news. </p>
<p><b>Sign up for a one year membership available at the following levels:</b></p>
<p><b>     Partner </b>($35-$149)</p>
<p><b>     Preserver </b>($150-$249)</p>
<p><b>     Steward </b>($250-$499)</p>
<p><b>     Guardian </b>($500-$999)</p>
<p><b>     Northern White Cedar Giving Circle </b>($1,000-$9,999)</p>
<p><b>     Grand Peninsula Society </b>($10,000+)</p>
<p><strong>     Bright Horizon Society </strong> ($20,000+)</p>
<b><br />
Two Ways to Join:</b>
<p>Annual Membership Contribution </p>
<p>To join, <a href="https://www.doorcountylandtrust.org/join-the-land-trust/">donate online</a> or send us your <a href="https://www.doorcountylandtrust.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/Membership_contribution.pdf" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">membership form</a> to make a donation at the membership level of your choice. Each year, you will receive a letter inviting you to renew your membership.</p>
<p>Evergreen Giving (Recurring Monthly Giving)</p>
<p><a href="https://www.doorcountylandtrust.org/join-the-land-trust/">Monthly giving</a> adds up! You can give your membership donation with a gift of any amount set to recur on a monthly basis.</p>
