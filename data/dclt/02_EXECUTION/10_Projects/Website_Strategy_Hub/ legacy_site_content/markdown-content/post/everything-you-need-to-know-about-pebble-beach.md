The Village of Sister Bay has entered into an agreement with the Door County Land Trust with the goal of purchasing and preserving Pebble Beach. The Luber and Downey families put the 17-acre parcel, including 600 feet of pristine shoreline, on the market for $3.6 million last year. The families lowered the price to $2.4 million to make the purchase possible. The deal will close in mid-September if the Land Trust is successful in raising $500,000 for the project.
