<span style="font-size: 14pt;">Itching to get your hands dirty and really connect with the land? Discover the rewarding feel of restoration work in the field! Join the upcoming work party and help with trail maintenance! The day will include using loppers and handsaws to clear the trail as well as bagging and removing any invasives</span><span style="font-size: 14pt;"> from the site. Volunteers will also get to spend time helping to clean-up the trail and with general preserve maintenance. People should dress for the weather and walking on uneven terrain (at least sturdy shoes and long pants). The Land Trust will bring all necessary supplies and equipment and of course our finest granola bars. Hope to see you there!</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Location: </strong>Bay Shore Blufflands Nature Preserve, 5519 Reynolds Road, County B (meet at the "upper" parking area)</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Date:</strong> Tuesday, July 23, 2019</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Time:</strong> 9:00 am - 12:00 am*</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;">* Please arrive on time. Work crews meet at the trailhead area, but may be difficult to locate after work had begun.</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>What to wear:</strong> Dress in layers for a variety of weather possibilities. Long sleeves and long pants are recommended.</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>What to bring:</strong> Water bottle, sunscreen, snacks, insect repellent. You may bring work gloves, goggles, kneepads or other personal gear if desired.</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Cancellation Policy:</strong> Work day plans are dependent on weather. Please check for possible cancellations or schedule updates on the <span style="color: #3366ff;"><a style="color: #3366ff;" href="https://doorstewardshipalliance.weebly.com/">Door Stewardship Alliance website</a></span>.</span>
