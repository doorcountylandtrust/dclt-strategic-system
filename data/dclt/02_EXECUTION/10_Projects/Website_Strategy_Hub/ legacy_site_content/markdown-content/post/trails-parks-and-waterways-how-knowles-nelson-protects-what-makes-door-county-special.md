<h2 class="mb-2 mt-6 text-lg font- first:mt-3">League of Women Voters to Host Talk on Knowles-Nelson Stewardship Program!</h2>
<p class="my-0">The League of Women Voters of Door County invites you to an informative evening exploring how the Knowles-Nelson Stewardship Program protects what makes Door County so special – our trails, parks, and waterways!</p>
<p class="my-0">Join us on <strong>April 7, 2025, at 6:30 PM</strong> at <strong>Crossroads at Big Creek</strong> for a presentation and discussion on this vital program. Learn how Knowles-Nelson helps preserve our natural treasures for generations to come.</p>

We hope to see you there!</p>
