THEY'RE BACK!!!! Every evening around this time of year, hundreds of sandhill cranes, ducks and other waterfowl descend onto the beautiful ponds on the Ivan and JoAnn Bissen property. Witnessing the evening return of these magnificent birds is indeed a spectacle to behold. This year's Annual Gathering at the 460-acre Bissen property will likely coincide with the birds' return. If you haven't already signed up to join us on Sunday, August 23rd there is still time.  Don't miss this opportunity to see nature at its finest and to celebrate land preservation efforts with friends and neighbors.

<strong>We hope to see you there!</strong>

From the staff and Board of Directors of the Door County Land Trust
