Tuesday, October 10, 10:00 am to 12:00 pm

Legacy Nature Preserve at Clay Banks, <a href="https://www.google.com/maps/place/1188+S+Lake+Michigan+Dr,+Sturgeon+Bay,+WI+54235/@44.7945174,-87.4118978,12z/data=!4m5!3m4!1s0x881d466191d9ddef:0xb78920164b4499bd!8m2!3d44.7301715!4d-87.3430268">1188 S Lake Michigan Dr</a>, Sturgeon Bay

Join the Door County Land Trust for a morning of work at Legacy Nature Preserve at Clay Banks. We will be using the cut-stump method to control non-native honeysuckle and autumn olive. Please dress appropriately for the weather and for the use of herbicide (long-sleeves, pants and sturdy shoes).

Please contact us to learn more at (920)746-1359 or volunteer@doorcountylandtrust.org.
