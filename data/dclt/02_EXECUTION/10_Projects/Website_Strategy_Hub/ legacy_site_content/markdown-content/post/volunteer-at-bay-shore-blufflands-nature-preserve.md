Join the fun at our next Door Stewardship Alliance work day  at Door County Land Trust’s Bay Shore Blufflands Nature Preserve on Tuesday, July 11<sup>th</sup>. The work will consist of clearing vegetation encroaching into the trail corridor as well as replacing parking area posts.

We will meet at the preserve parking area located at 5519 Reynolds Road at 9:00am and will work until approximately 12:00pm. Please wear sturdy shoes and dress appropriately for the weather. To get to the preserve parking area on Reynolds Road from Highway 42 in Carlsville, turn west onto Carlsville Road (County I). Travel 1.5 miles to Reynolds Road. Turn left and travel 0.7 miles to the preserve parking area located on the right-hand side of the road.

Door County Land Trust will supply all necessary equipment. Please contact the Door County Land Trust office with questions or if it is your first time volunteering for field work (920)746-1359.
