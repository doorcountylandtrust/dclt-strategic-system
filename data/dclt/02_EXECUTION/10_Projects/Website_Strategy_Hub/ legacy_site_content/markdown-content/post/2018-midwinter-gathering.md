Join members and friends for the beloved annual winter benefit, the Midwinter Gathering. Enjoy an evening filled with laughter, camaraderie and awe for this beautiful peninsula. The evening will close with a short program sharing inspiring stories of the past year and looking ahead to upcoming challenges and opportunities.</span>

<hr />

<span style="color: #333333; font-size: 14pt;"><span style="color: #ff0000;"><em><b>Register here starting January 10th!</b></em></span>
Tickets are $85 per person, with all proceeds benefitting our land protection efforts. Register online by February 14 or call our office at (920) 746-1359 to register by phone.</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><span style="color: #333333; font-size: 14pt;"><span style="color: #ff0000;"><em><b>Thank you!</b></em></span></span>
Proceeds from ticket sales help to support the Door County Land Trust’s land protection and restoration work. You will receive a receipt for the tax-deductible portion of your ticket price.</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><span style="color: #333333; font-size: 14pt;"><span style="color: #ff0000;"><em><b><span style="font-size: 18pt;">Evening Schedule!</span>
</b></em></span></span></span>
<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><b>4:30 - 6:00 pm</b></span>
<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><em>Social with wine, hors d’oeuvres, chef-carved Prime New York Strip, and delectable desserts home-made by friends of the Door County Land Trust.</em></span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><b>6:00 - 7:00 pm</b></span>
<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><em>Preview what’s ahead in 2018, including new lands, critical restoration of wildlife habitat, and a sneak peek at our outdoor adventures and outings.</em></span>
