<span style="font-size: 14pt;">Itching to get your hands dirty and really connect with the land? Discover the rewarding feel of restoration work in the field! Join the upcoming work party and help with invasive species removal! The day will include hand-pulling garlic mustard, bagging it and removing it from the site. Volunteers will also get to spend time on the shore and while helping to clean-up the beach and on the trails for some maintenance. People should dress for the weather and walking on uneven terrain (at least sturdy shoes and long pants). The Land Trust will bring all necessary supplies and equipment and of course our finest granola bars. Hope to see you there!</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Location: </strong>Sturgeon Bay Ship Canal Nature Preserve, 5200 Lake Lane, Sturgeon Bay</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Date:</strong> Tuesday, June 4, 2019</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Time:</strong> 9:00 am - 12:00 am*</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;">* Please arrive on time. Work crews meet at the trailhead area, but may be difficult to locate after work had begun.</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>What to wear:</strong> Dress in layers for a variety of weather possibilities. Long sleeves and long pants are recommended.</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>What to bring:</strong> Water bottle, sunscreen, snacks, insect repellent. You may bring work gloves, goggles, kneepads or other personal gear if desired.</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Cancellation Policy:</strong> Work day plans are dependent on weather. Please check for possible cancellations or schedule updates on the <span style="color: #3366ff;"><a style="color: #3366ff;" href="https://doorstewardshipalliance.weebly.com/">Door Stewardship Alliance website</a></span>.</span>
