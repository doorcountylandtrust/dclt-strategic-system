<span data-contrast="auto"><strong>Volunteer with the WI Bumble Bee Brigade! </strong></span>

<span data-contrast="auto">Help protect Wisconsin’s native bumble bees by becoming a community scientist with the Wisconsin Bumble Bee Brigade! </span><span data-contrast="none">The Bumble Bee Brigade (B3) is coordinated by the Wisconsin Department of Natural Resources and partners with volunteers from the public to conduct long-term monitoring of the state's native bumble bees. Volunteers collect data by taking photos of the bumble bee species they spot within a specified survey area. </span><span data-contrast="none">You can learn more about B3 by visiting the website: </span><a href="https://wiatri.net/inventory/BBB/aboutUs/"><span data-contrast="none">https://wiatri.net/inventory/BBB/aboutUs/</span></a><span data-contrast="none">. </span>

<span data-contrast="none">Door County Land Trust is seeking out volunteers to conduct surveys on DCLT managed properties once a week starting at the end of July and continuing through the end of August. </span><span data-ccp-props="{}"> </span>

<span data-contrast="none">If you are interested in conducting bumble bee surveys with the Door County Land Trust, please email Community Conservation Coordinator, Paige Witek, at </span><a href="mailto:pwitek@doorcountylandtrust.org"><span data-contrast="none">pwitek@doorcountylandtrust.org</span></a><span data-contrast="none"> for more information.</span><span data-ccp-props="{&quot;201341983&quot;:0,&quot;335557856&quot;:16777215,&quot;335559739&quot;:100,&quot;335559740&quot;:240}"> </span>
