<span style="font-family: 'quattrocento sans'; font-size: 14pt;">I’ve decided to stop asking myself if the snow will end. Instead, I am embracing this mid-April snowstorm. In fact, based on the <a href="https://www.doorcountylandtrust.org/bay-shore-blufflands-buckthorn-blast/">Bay Shore Blufflands restoration project</a>, I should be celebrating this cold spring for giving us more time to treat the invasive buckthorn. But, as you’ll soon read, spring will come and our <a href="https://secure.doorcountylandtrust.org/events-registration-merchandise/events/category/explore-the-door/">upcoming hikes</a> provide some great opportunities to shake off winter and experience Door County in all of her thawing beauty.</span>

<hr />

<span style="font-family: 'quattrocento sans'; font-size: 14pt;"><a href="https://www.doorcountylandtrust.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/2018-Wild-Words-8pt5x11-Calling-All-Storytellers.pdf"><img class="alignright wp-image-4797 size-medium" src="https://www.doorcountylandtrust.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/WW_horizontal-300x110.png" alt="Wild Words" width="300" height="110" /></a>Storytellers, writers, poets, rappers... you are welcome to participate in the April 26 <a href="https://www.doorcountylandtrust.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/2018-Wild-Words-8pt5x11-Calling-All-Storytellers.pdf" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Wild Words</a> program. Contact us to find out more about how to share your story and pictures in 6 minutes and 40 seconds. That's what Wild Words is all about!</span>
