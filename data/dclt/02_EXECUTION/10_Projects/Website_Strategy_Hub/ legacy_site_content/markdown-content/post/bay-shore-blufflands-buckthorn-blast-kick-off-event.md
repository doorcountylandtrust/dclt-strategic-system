<span style="font-size: 14pt;">Join the neighborhood effort to remove invasive buckthorn and honeysuckle!</span>

<span style="font-size: 14pt;">These wicked weeds are a real ‘thorn’ in our side. Here’s why:</span>
<ul>
 	<li><span style="font-family: 'quattrocento sans'; font-size: 14pt;">They shade out native plants like trillium and Lady’s slipper orchids.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-family: 'quattrocento sans'; font-size: 14pt;">Their berries cause nutrient deficiencies for birds and wildlife.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-family: 'quattrocento sans'; font-size: 14pt;">Each berry carries four seeds which are easily spread to neighbors’ yards by birds.</span></li>
 	<li><span style="font-family: 'quattrocento sans'; font-size: 14pt;">They contribute to soil erosion.</span></li>
</ul>
<span style="font-size: 14pt;">Attend the Buckthorn Blast Kick-Off to join our efforts! The first 40 participants receive a free Buckthorn Blast kit.</span>
