Woodwalk Gallery, south of Egg Harbor

<span style="font-size: 16pt;"><strong><a href="https://secure.doorcountylandtrust.org/events-registration-merchandise/event/pass-the-paella-dinner-and-concert-with-julian-hagen-and-friends/">View details and register online</a></strong></span>

<span style="font-size: 16pt;">Join us for a special dinner and concert—one night only—to benefit the Door County Land Trust’s land protection efforts. Events like these show how the nature of Door County infuses every aspect of art, music and local life. Musicians bring such emotion to the beauty all around us—no one will leave the show without a greater appreciation for the land and waters of this incredible peninsula.</span>

<span style="font-size: 16pt;">Julian Hagen is a fifth generation Washington Island native whose lyrics are infused with island life and lore. Hagen will be joined by Memphis musicians Tommy Burroughs and Eddie Smith, as well as percussionist Kevin Jones.</span>

<a href="https://secure.doorcountylandtrust.org/events-registration-merchandise/event/pass-the-paella-dinner-and-concert-with-julian-hagen-and-friends/"><img class="wp-image-6041 size-medium alignleft" style="display: block;" src="https://secure.doorcountylandtrust.org/events-registration-merchandise/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/Tomy-Burrooughs-and-Eddie-Smith_web-225x300.png" sizes="(max-width: 225px) 100vw, 225px" srcset="https://secure.doorcountylandtrust.org/events-registration-merchandise/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/Tomy-Burrooughs-and-Eddie-Smith_web-225x300.png 225w, https://secure.doorcountylandtrust.org/events-registration-merchandise/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/Tomy-Burrooughs-and-Eddie-Smith_web-68x90.png 68w, https://secure.doorcountylandtrust.org/events-registration-merchandise/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/Tomy-Burrooughs-and-Eddie-Smith_web-113x150.png 113w, https://secure.doorcountylandtrust.org/events-registration-merchandise/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/Tomy-Burrooughs-and-Eddie-Smith_web.png 413w" alt="" width="225" height="300" /></a>

<span style="font-size: 16pt;">Toast with a glass of sangria and enjoy antipasto to begin the evening. A buffet of paella, fresh salads, gazpacho and fresh bread will be served at 6pm, with the concert beginning at 7pm.</span>

<span style="font-size: 16pt;">Many thanks to generous sponsorships from Jillaine Burton Seefeldt and Andrew Seefeldt, owners of Woodwalk Gallery, and Scott McEvoy, chef and owner of McEvoy’s Culinaria Catering. Sangria, wine, beverages and desserts are sponsored by members of the Land Trust and our board of directors, Deanna D'Abbraccio and Barb Morgan, Donna and Tony DeNardo, and Barb and Jeff Ottum.</span>

<span style="font-size: 16pt; color: #008000;"><em><b>All proceeds from this event support the Door County Land Trust’s land protection and stewardship efforts.</b></em></span>
