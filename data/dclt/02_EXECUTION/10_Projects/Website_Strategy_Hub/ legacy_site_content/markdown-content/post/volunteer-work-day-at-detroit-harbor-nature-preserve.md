<span style="font-family: 'quattrocento sans'; font-size: 14pt;">We are continuing our efforts to better establish the new trail at Detroit Harbor Nature Preserve. Please meet at 600 Lobdell Point Road at 10:30am on Tuesday, September 11. We need help clearing vegetation from the trail using loppers and help marking the trail with Land Trust trail markers. Once this is complete, we will work on a section of the Richter Forest trail. This work is creating a trail system by connecting the new Detroit Harbor trail, the Town of Washington trail and the Richter Forest trail.</span>
