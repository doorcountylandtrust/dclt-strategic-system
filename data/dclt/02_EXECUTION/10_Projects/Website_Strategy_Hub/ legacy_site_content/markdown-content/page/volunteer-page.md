<p><em><strong>"We retired to Door County because we'd had enough 'pavement pounding' and loss of nature in our earlier lives. So when the chance to preserve the canal property occurred, it was a no-brainer - do whatever possible to save this splendid place. Thanks to the Land Trust and many, many dedicated folks, the Ship Canal Nature Preserve has become and will remain a public treasure." </strong></em></p>
<p><strong>- Bill Utley, Preserve Steward and avid hiker</strong></p>
<hr />
<p><b>Your Time Makes a Big Difference</b></p>
<p>Volunteers are vital to the Land Trust's mission. They infuse energy and new ideas into exciting projects and events and serve a key role in community engagement. Volunteer work includes stewarding the land and preparing nature preserves for public use, assisting with events, serving as a representative of the Land Trust at festivals, administrative tasks, and much more.</p>
<p>We are indebted to our volunteers' commitment to protecting Door County's exceptional lands and waters since the Land Trust's founding in 1986.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><b>Volunteer Opportunities</b></p>
<p>Whether you devote a single afternoon or several hours a month, the Land Trust appreciates your contribution. We'll partner your skills and interests with a task that needs care.</p>
<p><strong>Ambassador at Booth Event -</strong> Assist with hosting a booth at community events to help us spread the word about the importance of land protection and stewardship. </p>
<p><strong>Citizen Science (Field Research) -</strong> Collect data on the flora and fauna on the preserves to better inform land stewardship decisions.</p>
<p><strong>Filing or Data Entry -</strong> Assist with administrative office tasks.</p>
<p><strong>Hike Leading -</strong> Lead, co-lead, or assist with guiding groups on our nature preserves. </p>
<p><strong>Mailing Parties -</strong> Mail event invitations, newsletters, and membership renewals with a group. </p>
<p><strong>Outdoor Work and Land Stewardship -</strong> Remove invasive plants, blaze and maintain trails, and make general repairs and improvements to our nature preserves. </p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><b>Interested in Volunteering?</b></p>
<p>To sign-up to volunteer with the Door County Land Trust, <a href="https://www.doorcountylandtrust.org/contact-us/"><strong>CLICK HERE</strong></a>. This link will bring you to our 'Contact Us' page. Check the box next to 'Volunteer' and a form will appear for you to enter more information about yourself and your volunteer interests. </p>
