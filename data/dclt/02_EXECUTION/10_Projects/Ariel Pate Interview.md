Ariel donated the land to honor her father, Jon "Fritz" Renner.

1. What does this land mean to you personally?
	•	How long has it been in your life?
	•	What memories come to mind when you think of it?

2. Could you share a favorite memory of your father on this land?
	•	What did he love most about the place?
	•	Are there particular activities, seasons, or traditions you shared there?

3. What made you decide to donate the land in his memory?
	•	Was this something you discussed with him?
	•	Was there a moment when it just felt like the right thing to do?

4. What qualities did your father have that you see reflected in the land—or in your decision?
	•	(e.g., quiet strength, resilience, stewardship, joy in nature…)

5. How do you hope the land will be experienced or remembered by others now that it’s protected?
	•	Do you imagine people walking there, learning from it, feeling peace?

6. What do you want people to understand about the importance of protecting land like this?
	•	Why is conservation important to you personally?

7. Is there anything else you’d like to share about your father, your land, or your hopes for its future?
	•	Any stories, emotions, or thoughts you’d like to include?

8. Sometimes it’s hard to put it into words, but if you were just talking to a friend about what this land means—what would you say?


Narrative Elicitation Questions
Can you tell me what this land means to you personally?

When you are on this land, what do you notice, feel, or think about most?

What inspired you to make this gift, and what do you hope it will carry forward into the future?

How do you imagine this land being cared for or experienced by future generations?

What qualities or values guided your decision to donate the land?

If this land could tell a story, what story do you think it would tell?

How would you describe the kind of legacy you hope this gift represents?

Are there particular places, features, or memories connected with this property that hold special significance to you?

When you think about your journey toward making this decision, what moments stand out?

Questions with Optional Father Connection
You’ve chosen to make this gift in memory of your father. What feels most meaningful to you about honoring him in this way?

Are there values, lessons, or pieces of wisdom from your father that you see reflected in this gift?

If you’d like, could you share a story about your father that comes to mind when you think of this land?

What do you think your father would feel if he could see this land preserved today?
